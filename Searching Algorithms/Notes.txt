What is the advantage of binary search over linear search
Comparison of an array having 1000 elements

🌸LINEAR SEARCH - Would take 1000 comparisons

🌸BINARY SEARCH
1000 ➡️ 500 ➡️ 250 ➡️ 125 ➡️ 62 ➡️ 31 ➡️ 15 ➡️ 7 ➡️ 3 ➡️ 1 ➡️ 0

That is, considering the worst case, linear search would take 1000 comparisons, while binary search takes only 10 !!
